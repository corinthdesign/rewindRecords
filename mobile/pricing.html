Pricing

Rewind Records is a locally owned and operated recording studio in Colorado Springs. We are well versed in the entire recording process, from pre-production to post-production; we focus on every step and aspect of a recording project to build intricate sounds and transform your project into tracks that slam!

We know that most musicians on a local level work with a tight budget, especially when it comes to recording. That's why It's our goal to provide professional quality recordings to musicians at a fair price thats reasonable to work around.

Bands

Standard Rates

  • Hourly Rates: $30 per hour (Min. of 2)

  • Mixing: $160 per song

  • Mastering: $200 per song

  • Mix & Master Bundle: $325

  • Full Song Rate: $350 per song (Includes 6 hours recording time per song plus Mix and Master)

Project Rates

  • The EP: 4 to 7 Songs: $300 per song

  • The Album: 8+ Songs: $280 per song

Referral Rates

To any artists who record a track with Rewind and then refer an artist who also records with Rewind, the referring artist will be given a $100 credit towards any new production! (combined with monthly promotions on a case by case basis)

First-time Artist Discount

Any new artist who records with us will receive a 20% discount off their first track!
